Rocket League was released in July, 2015 to widespread acclaim. The tightly-optimized gameplay loop and fun physics engine hinted to many of us that the game could offer so much more. In 2016, I was a part of some of the earliest waves of modders attempting to expand the functionality of the game with training tools and new game modes. Through the process of learning to create custom game modes in the Unreal Development Kit (UDK or UE3), I compiled a 100-page Google doc explaining the process, which eventually became [RocketLeagueMapmaking.com](/project.html?id=rlmm-com) and led to the [Rocket League Mapmaking Discord Server](/project.html?id=rlmm-discord).

# Spotlight
Nearly all of my projects are available on the [Steam Workshop](https://steamcommunity.com/id/dasoccerguy/myworkshopfiles/?appid=252950). Here are some that I'm the most proud of:

## Bucket Park
![Bucket Park](img/project/rl-custom/bucketpark.jpeg)

In collaboration with AGW from TheHoopSquad, I designed a new standard Hoops map. While I don't have concrete proof, I think this project provided inspiration for an [official map which was added a few years later](https://www.rocketleague.com/news/turn-up-the-block-with-a-new-hoops-arena).

## Panic's Air Race
![Air Race](img/project/rl-custom/airrace.jpeg)

In collaboration with Panic, I helped him bring to life a new game mode. For over a year, weekly race events were livestreamed on Twitch.

## RFH Stadium
![RFH](img/project/rl-custom/rfh.jpeg)

Since 2017, I have been a moderator and admin for [Ranked Hoops](/project.html?id=ranked-hoops). This is a standard Hoops map set in an epic stadium, featuring banners for the winners of all of our most notable tournaments.

## Kidney Island
![Kidney Island](img/project/rl-custom/kidney.jpeg)

In August, 2021 I produced a map for a massive-multiplayer fundraiser event. $2500 was raised for the National Kidney Foundation in honor of Danny Oganovich and his ongoing battle with kidney disease.

## FaZe Luxodrome
![FaZe](img/project/rl-custom/faze.jpeg)

For the 2023 RLCS Major in Copenhagen I built a challenge map which was featured at the FaZe Clan booth. Over two days, players competed to establish the best time on the course to win prizes.

## NeoTokyo Haxxelerator
![haxx](img/project/rl-custom/haxx.jpeg)

In early 2025 I released a challenge map which was featured in the [Rocket League GDQ Showcase](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iMft32TvjRg&t=44m44s).

# Events
In addition to solo efforts, I have been a part of a wide range of collaborative efforts to produce maps for community events, both online and in-person:

## Gold Rush Renaissance Ruckus
[![rr](img/project/rl-custom/rr.jpeg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KzezgIXvKJY)

In summer 2021, I collaborated with Lethamyr to produce five maps for an online, themed tournament featuring pros and content creators. We reskinned four community maps in a Middle Ages theme, and produced a brand new game mode for the grand finale.

## Dacia Spring Electric Challenge
[![dsec](img/project/rl-custom/dsec.jpeg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AHXzuplWMHY)

The community competed for €2000 in prize money for two weeks in April, 2022 in a map sponsored by Dacia to promote their new EV. Gidek, Lethamyr, and I created a grueling, 20-level speed challenge set in a range of environments.

## Gold Rush Totally Excellent Weekend
[![tew](img/project/rl-custom/tew.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T1V1Cx_wlDE)

In August 2022, Wisdom Gaming hosted a LAN event at their venue in the Mall of America in Minneapolis. Simple Shark and I created six arcade-themed maps, on which 12 professional players and streamers battled for a substantial cash prize.

## Haunted Hallows Escape
[![hh](img/project/rl-custom/hhpuzzle4_1.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_ugZ1PGpuKs)

Working with Lethamyr and Simple Shark, we produced a four-part escape room for Halloween 2022 which was officially sponsored by the creators of the game (a first). It featured four "anteggonists" from scary movie franchises which had partnered with Epic Games for an in-game event. 8 prominent streamers raced to complete the map.

## Immortals Progressive Tower
[![improgto](img/project/rl-custom/improgto.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KHQ2_Q2_4IA)

Lethamyr, Simple Shark, and I worked for months to produce a community challenge map released in July 2023. Progressive Insurance and the esports org Immortals sponsored a month-long race for the best time on 20 floors of challenges.

## Immortals Progressive Island
[![ipi](img/project/rl-custom/ipi.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nuKraZ7f-RQ)

A successor to the Tower, Lethamyr, Simple Shark, and I produced a Diddy Kong Racing-inspired open world challenge map for a $5000 contest in September 2024. Featuring eight (nine?) unique biomes and dozens of easter eggs, this is by far our most ambitious project.

## The Apex
[![ipi](img/project/rl-custom/apex.jpg)](https://liquipedia.net/rocketleague/Lethamyr/Lawler/The_Apex)

In December 2024, Lethamyr and Lawler hosted a $10,000 LAN at Lethamyr's home in Canada. We reskinned 12 community maps in a gritty, nighttime aesthetic, and 12 professional players and content creators competed for their share of the prize over two days.
