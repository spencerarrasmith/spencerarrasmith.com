# Esports Coach

From 2020 through the end of 2025, I served as the Esports coach for Pinnacle Charter High School in Federal Heights, CO. This is a CHSAA-sanctioned after-school activity and had a peak membership of 12 students. Matches and scrimmages are coordinated through [PlayVS.](https://playvs.com/)

The League of Legends team (above) finished 3rd in the state in spring 2023.
